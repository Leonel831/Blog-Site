Why should we care about the environment?

"We won't have a society if we destroy the environment"

Biodiversity

It is essential for a healthy ecosystem that provide food and air.

Quality of Life

A healthy environment provide us with clean air, water, food, materials, and space for recreation allowing us to thrive and live a comfortable life.

Mental Health

Being in nature can promote relaxation, restoration, and a sense of calmness. It can also reduce stress, increase positive emotions, and improve concentration

Did you know that ...

Around 27,000 trees are cut down every day. At the current rate of deforestation, 5-10% of tropical forest species will become extinct every decade.
